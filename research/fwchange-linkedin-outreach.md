**Run Tests**: 50 messages per variation, measure response rate, iterate

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do ‚úÖ

- **Do** personalize every message (mention their company, vendor, title)
- **Do** lead with credibility (25 years experience)
- **Do** quantify results ("40% faster", "8-12 hours/week saved")
- **Do** make it easy to respond (one-click calendar link)
- **Do** respect their time (offer opt-out)
- **Do** follow up 2-3 times (most responses come after follow-up)
- **Do** add value in every post (no hard sells)

### Don't ‚ùå

- **Don't** send generic copy-paste messages (gets ignored)
- **Don't** be pushy or salesy (kills relationships)
- **Don't** follow up more than 3 times (becomes spam)
- **Don't** pitch on first connection request (build rapport first)
- **Don't** use LinkedIn InMail for cold outreach (save for warm 2nd connections)
- **Don't** forget to track metrics (can't improve what you don't measure)
- **Don't** give up after Week 1 (outbound takes consistency)
