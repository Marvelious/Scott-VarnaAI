## Research Methodology

### Market Sizing
1. **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: Total market demand
2. **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: Segment we can serve
3. **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: Realistic capture in 1-3 years

### Competitive Analysis
1. **Direct Competitors**: Same solution, same market
2. **Indirect Competitors**: Different solution, same problem
3. **Substitutes**: Alternative approaches to problem

### Customer Research
1. **Persona Development**: Ideal customer profiles
2. **Pain Point Analysis**: Problems worth solving
3. **Willingness to Pay**: Pricing research

## Strategic Frameworks

### Product-Market Fit
- **Problem/Solution Fit**: Does our solution solve the problem?
- **Product/Market Fit**: Does the market want our solution?
- **Business Model Fit**: Can we profitably deliver it?

### Go-to-Market Strategy
1. **Target Segment**: Who to sell to first
2. **Channel Strategy**: How to reach them
3. **Pricing Strategy**: What to charge
4. **Sales Process**: How to close deals

### Competitive Positioning
- **Differentiation**: What makes us unique
- **Value Proposition**: Why customers should choose us
- **Competitive Moats**: What protects our market position
