## TOPIC 2: BULGARIAN COMPLIANCE MARKET

### 2.1 NIS2 Directive Implementation Status

#### Current Status: **NON-COMPLIANT**

**EU Deadline**: October 17, 2024
**Bulgaria Status**: **MISSED DEADLINE**

**Timeline**:
- August 2024: Draft Law published for public consultation
- November 28, 2024: EU opened infringement procedures against Bulgaria + 22 other states
- December 11, 2024: Council of Ministers approved draft
- February 20, 2025: Adopted at first reading in Parliament
- May 7, 2025: EU sent reasoned opinion for failure to notify full transposition
- **Mid-2026**: Expected full implementation with 10,000-12,000 entities in scope

**Legal Risk**: Bulgaria is in violation of EU law with open criminal proceedings

**Source**: [ECSO NIS2 Tracker](https://ecs-org.eu/activities/nis2-directive-transposition-tracker/) | [NIS2 Bulgaria Status](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/nis2-directive-bulgaria)

---

#### Bulgarian NIS2 Framework

**Primary Legislation**: Cybersecurity Act (2018, as amended)

**Planned Ordinances** (2 separate):
1. **General Ordinance**: Minimum cybersecurity measures for all in-scope entities (excluding telecom and DORA-regulated entities)
2. **Telecom Ordinance**: Specific measures for public electronic communications network/service providers

**Interim Framework**: Existing Ordinance on Minimum Requirements for Network and Information Security remains in effect until new ordinances adopted

**Source**: [Eversheds Sutherland Bulgaria NIS2](https://ezine.eversheds-sutherland.com/eu-nis2-directive/bulgaria)

---

#### Governance Structure: Distributed Model

**Main Supervisory Authority**: Ministry of Electronic Governance (MEGA - Bulgarian acronym SEGA)

**Sector-Specific Regulators**:
- Energy and Water Regulatory Commission (EWRC)
- Bulgarian National Bank (BNB)
- Sector authorities for transport, healthcare, etc.

**Council of Ministers Role**: Designates separate supervisory authority for each NIS2 sector

**Registration Portal**: SEGA (MEGA) expected to launch H2 2025

**Bulgaria-Specific Exclusion**: Local level public administration NOT included in draft legislation

**Source**: [IDVKM NIS2 Bulgaria](https://idvkm.com/en/2024/11/07/nis2-in-bulgaria-what-do-companies-need-to-know/) | [Copla NIS2 Bulgaria](https://copla.com/blog/compliance-regulations/nis2-directive-regulations-and-implementation-in-bulgaria/)

8. **Digital Infrastructure**: DNS providers, TLD registries, cloud computing, data centers, CDNs, trust service providers, public electronic communications networks
9. **Public Administration**: Central government agencies (local administration excluded in Bulgaria)

### 2.3 Penalties for NIS2 Non-Compliance

**Essential Entities (High Criticality)**:
- **€10 million** OR
- **2% of total worldwide annual turnover**
- Whichever is higher

**Important Entities (Other Critical Sectors)**:
- **€7 million** OR
- **1.4% of total worldwide annual turnover**
- Whichever is higher

**Additional Sanctions**:
- Administrative inspections
- Suspension of executives for serious violations

**Source**: [Copla NIS2 Penalties](https://copla.com/blog/compliance-regulations/nis2-directive-regulations-and-implementation-in-bulgaria/)

### 2.4 GDPR Enforcement Landscape

**Official Name**: Комисия за защита на личните данни (КЗЛД)
