# SEO Keyword Sales Optimization - Q1 2025

## Project Overview

**Objective**: Transform SEO keywords across 5 WordPress sites from traffic-focused to sales-conversion-focused keywords.

**Problem Statement**: Current Rank Math focus keywords optimize for informational/awareness traffic (high SEO scores 74-94/100) but have LOW buyer intent. Legal pages consume 40%+ of SEO effort with ZERO sales potential. Missing critical decision-stage keywords ("Demo", "Preis", "kaufen", "Angebot").

**Business Impact**: Estimated €115K-490K/year revenue potential from keyword optimization.

## Sites Priority Order (by Revenue Potential)

1. **ai-projektmanager.de** - Highest value German B2B market, NIS2 urgency
2. **varnaai.com** - Portfolio showcase, demo conversion path
3. **varna-agenten.de** - Creative agency AI tools market
4. **aimarketingbg.com** - Bulgarian/English market expansion
5. **classicsecurity.net** - Security services lead generation

## Requirements

#### varnaai.com (14 pages)

- Change "Enterprise AI" → "Enterprise AI Solutions Pricing"

#### varna-agenten.de (19 pages)
- Change "KI Agenten" → "KI Agenten für Agenturen kaufen"
- Change "Automatisierung" → "KI Automatisierung Demo anfragen"

#### aimarketingbg.com (13 pages)

#### classicsecurity.net (pending audit)
- Audit current keywords
- Transform to "Security Services Quote", "Firewall Migration Demo"

### Phase 2: Content Optimization (Week 3-4)

For each site:
- Rewrite H1/H2 headings with transactional keywords
- Add CTAs with "Demo", "Kostenlos testen", "Angebot anfragen"
- Create internal link clusters to pricing/demo pages
- Update meta descriptions with urgency (NIS2 deadline, limited spots)

### Phase 3: New High-Intent Pages (Month 2)

Create new pages per site:
- /demo or /kostenlose-demo
- /preise-vergleich or /pricing-calculator
- /roi-rechner (ROI calculator)
- /case-study-[industry] with conversion forms
