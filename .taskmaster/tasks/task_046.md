# Task ID: 46

**Title:** Hire Bulgarian Business Development Representative for AI Marketing BG

**Status:** in-progress

**Dependencies:** 44

**Priority:** high

**Description:** Recruit and onboard a part-time Bulgarian BD representative (20 hrs/week, €800-1200/mo + 10-15% commission) to lead market entry and sales activities in Bulgaria. This hire is critical for unblocking all Bulgaria market expansion initiatives.

**Details:**

This is a non-technical but critical operational task blocking Bulgaria market activities. Big Dick cannot execute Bulgaria sales alone without Bulgarian language/cultural expertise.

**Recruitment Phase (Week 1-2):**
1. Job posting creation:
   - Post on jobs.bg (primary Bulgarian job board)
   - Post on LinkedIn with Bulgarian language option
   - Post in 3-5 Facebook groups for Bulgarian professionals (tourism/hospitality/business communities)
   - Position: "Part-time Business Development Representative - AI Marketing Services"
   - Requirements: Fluent Bulgarian, tourism/hospitality background preferred, sales experience, strong network in Bulgarian SME market

2. Target candidate profile:
   - 5-10 qualified candidates with tourism/hospitality background (comfort with relationship-based selling)
   - Preference for candidates with existing SME networks in Varna region
   - Sales/commission-based compensation model experience
   - Understanding of Bulgarian business culture and decision-making processes

3. Interview process:
   - Conduct 5-10 initial interviews screening for language, cultural fit, sales aptitude
   - Second round interviews with Big Dick (via video call with translator if needed)
   - Reference checks focusing on relationship-building and sales capability
   - Trial project: Have finalist create list of 10-15 potential customer leads

**Onboarding Phase (Week 2-3):**
1. Contract preparation:
   - Structure: 20 hours/week minimum, €800-1200/month base + 10-15% commission on closed deals
   - Payment: Monthly via bank transfer (ensure Bulgaria-compatible payment setup)
   - Commission structure: Clear definition of what qualifies for commission (demo completed, contract signed, etc.)
   - Confidentiality and non-compete clauses (Bulgaria labor law compliant)

2. Sales materials preparation:
   - Create Bulgarian-language sales deck for AI Marketing BG services
   - Prepare customer success stories/case studies (anonymized, compliance-safe)
   - Create pricing sheets and proposal templates in Bulgarian
   - Develop email sequences for prospecting and follow-up
   - Create product demo walkthrough guide

3. Training and onboarding:
   - Weekly sync calls (schedule timezone-friendly times)
   - Product training on AI Marketing BG capabilities and pricing
   - Market education: Bulgarian SME landscape, competitive landscape, NIS2/GDPR compliance drivers
   - Sales process training: Lead qualification, demo scheduling, closing process
   - CRM/tracking setup (e.g., HubSpot or Pipedrive for pipeline management)

**Operational Setup:**
1. Communication infrastructure:
   - Establish preferred communication channels (Slack, WhatsApp, or Bulgarian-standard tools)
   - Create weekly reporting cadence (leads generated, demos scheduled, deals closed)
   - Set up shared calendar for availability/scheduling

2. Success metrics definition:
   - Leads generated per week (target: 15-20)
   - Demo requests scheduled per week (target: 5-7)
   - Conversion rate targets (target: 10-15% of demos → trials)
   - Revenue attribution (for commission calculation)

3. Market feedback collection:
   - Debrief sessions to understand Bulgarian market objections and needs
   - Gather intelligence on competitor positioning in Bulgaria
   - Document customer feedback for product development priorities

3. **Onboarding Completion:**

4. **Operational Handoff:**

5. **Performance Validation (After 2 weeks):**
