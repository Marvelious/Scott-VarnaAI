## Qualification Criteria (Before Offering Free Audit)

**Qualify**:
- Company size: 10-500 employees
- Industry: Tech, finance, healthcare (regulated)
- Budget indicator: "We're considering..." (not "We can't afford...")
- Decision maker: Owner, CTO, or has budget authority

**Disqualify**:
- "Can you do this for free?" (tire kicker)
- Solo entrepreneur (too small)
- "We'll get back to you in 6 months" (no urgency)
