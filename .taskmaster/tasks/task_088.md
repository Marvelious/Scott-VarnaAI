## Description

## Templates to Create

### 1. Discovery Call Script
**Sections**:
- Pre-call preparation checklist
- Introduction (5 min)
- Discovery questions (15 min)
  - "What's your biggest challenge with [AI/security/compliance]?"
  - "Have you considered AI but held back because of GDPR concerns?"
  - "What would solving [X] mean for your business?"
- Value demonstration (10 min) - relevant case study
- Next steps (10 min) - propose audit or project
- Qualification criteria checklist

**Format**: Google Doc
**Length**: 2-3 pages

### 2. Proposal Templates (3 Tiers)

**Entry Tier Proposal** (€980 Audit):
- Scope: Compliance audit (ISO 27001, GDPR, or NIS2)
- Deliverable: 15-20 page PDF report with findings + action plan
- Timeline: 5-7 business days
- Payment: 100% upfront
- Includes: 30-minute findings review call + free re-scan after 3 months

**Project Tier Proposal** (€4,900-€9,800):
- Scope: [Customizable - ISO implementation, Azure AI setup, firewall migration]
- Deliverable: Implementation + documentation + training
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- Payment: 50% deposit, 50% on delivery
- Includes: Weekly progress updates + 30 days post-delivery support

**Retainer Tier Proposal** (€980/month):
- Scope: 8 hours/month ongoing security advisory
- Services: Monthly security news digest, quarterly reviews, priority support
- Timeline: Month-to-month, 30-day notice to cancel
- Payment: Monthly invoice, due within 15 days
- Includes: <24 hour response time for questions

**Format**: Google Docs (easy customization per client)
