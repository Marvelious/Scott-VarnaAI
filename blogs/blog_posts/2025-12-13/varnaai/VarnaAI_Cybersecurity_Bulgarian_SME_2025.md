<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Essential Cybersecurity Measures for Bulgarian SMEs</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cybersecurity Bulgarian SME protection requires a layered approach. Implementing these fundamental controls significantly reduces your risk exposure:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. Employee Security Awareness Training</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Your employees represent both your greatest vulnerability and strongest defense. Regular training helps staff recognize phishing attempts, social engineering tactics, and suspicious activities. Training should be ongoing, not a one-time event.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cybersecurity Bulgarian SME strategies must include MFA for all critical systems. Even if passwords are compromised, MFA prevents unauthorized access. Implement MFA for email, financial systems, and remote access at minimum.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. Regular Software Updates and Patching</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Unpatched software contains known vulnerabilities that attackers actively exploit. Establish a regular patching schedule for operating systems, applications, and firmware. Automate updates where possible to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. Data Backup and Recovery</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Maintain regular backups following the 3-2-1 rule: three copies of data, on two different media types, with one copy stored offsite. Test backup restoration regularly to ensure data can actually be recovered when needed.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. Network Security Controls</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Implement firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and network segmentation. Separate critical systems from general network traffic. Monitor network activity for suspicious patterns indicating potential breaches.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
