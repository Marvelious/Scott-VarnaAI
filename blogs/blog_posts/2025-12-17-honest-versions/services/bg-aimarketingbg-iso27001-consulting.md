<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Защо техническата експертиза е критична за ISO 27001</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>ISO 27001 Annex A изисква реална техническа имплементация, не Word документи:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>A.13.1 Network security</strong>: Трябва deploying на firewalls, segmentation, IDS/IPS - не описание</li>
<li><strong>A.10.1 Cryptographic controls</strong>: Actual encryption implementation (TLS 1.3, AES-256) в production</li>
<li><strong>A.12.4 Logging</strong>: Working SIEM система със retention, не policy "ще логваме events"</li>
<li><strong>A.9.4 Access control</strong>: MFA deployed за всички users, RBAC configured, не "планираме да внедрим"</li>
<li><strong>A.16.1 Incident management</strong>: Automated alerting, runbooks, tested incident response - не untested procedures</li>
</ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
