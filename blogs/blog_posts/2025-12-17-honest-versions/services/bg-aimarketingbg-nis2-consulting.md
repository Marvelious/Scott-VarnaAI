<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Какво NIS2 директивата реално изисква</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>NIS2 Directive (EU) 2022/2555 въвежда задължителни cyber security мерки за critical и important entities:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Risk management framework</strong>: Documented cyber risk assessment и mitigation strategy</li>
<li><strong>Incident handling</strong>: 24-hour notification за significant incidents, detailed incident reporting</li>
<li><strong>Business continuity & crisis management</strong>: Backup systems, disaster recovery, crisis communication plans</li>
<li><strong>Supply chain security</strong>: Third-party risk assessment, vendor security requirements</li>
<li><strong>Network & information system security</strong>: Firewalls, encryption, access control, monitoring</li>
<li><strong>Vulnerability disclosure</strong>: Responsible disclosure procedures за security vulnerabilities</li>
<li><strong>Security awareness training</strong>: Mandatory cyber security training за всички employees</li>
<li><strong>Multi-factor authentication</strong>: MFA за critical systems и privileged accounts</li>
</ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Според <a href="https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/nis2-directive" target="_blank" rel="noopener">EC официалната NIS2 страница</a>, държавите членки трябваше да транспонират директивата до октомври 2024 - България има legislation в процес на приемане.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NIS2 глоби и enforcement в България</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>NIS2 Article 34 максимални глоби:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Essential entities</strong>: €10,000,000 или 2% от global annual turnover</li>
<li><strong>Important entities</strong>: €7,000,000 или 1.4% от global annual turnover</li>
<li><strong>Management liability</strong>: Personal liability за senior management при non-compliance</li>
</ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Според <a href="https://www.enisa.europa.eu/topics/cybersecurity-policy/nis-directive-new" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ENISA NIS2 guidance</a>, enforcement ще включва regular audits, incident investigations и potential penalties за systematic non-compliance.</p>
