<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Difference 1: User Intent and Behavior Patterns</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>GEO vs SEO begins with fundamentally different user behaviors. Traditional search users expect to browse multiple results, clicking through to websites for information. AI search users expect synthesized answers delivered directly, often without clicking anything.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>SEO optimizes for click-through rates and on-page engagement. Users land on your website, consume content, and hopefully convert. The website is the destination. GEO optimizes for citation and inclusion in AI responses. Your brand gets mentioned directly in the answer, building trust and awareness without requiring a click.</p>
