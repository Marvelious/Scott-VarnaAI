### 5. Free Compliance Assessment Offer

**What You Deliver** (30-min meeting):
1. Run C3 60-second scan on their domain
2. Explain what NIS2 means for them specifically
3. Show gap analysis (what's missing)
4. Give them a 1-page summary (free, no obligation)

**Conversion Goal**:
- Free assessment → Paid full audit (€500-1,000)
- Full audit → Monthly managed service (€100-200/mo)

## Notes

- **Bulgarian business hours**: 9:00-18:00, avoid calling Friday afternoon
- **Decision makers**: Often owners in SMEs, not IT managers
- **Trust building**: Multiple touchpoints before sale (expect 2-3 meetings)
- **Payment**: Bulgarian companies prefer bank transfer, not card
