## Strategic Pivot Summary

**FROM**: SaaS product sales (4 apps, ‚Ç¨10K MRR target)
**TO**: Consulting services first, SaaS demos later

**Rationale**:
- Bulgarian SMEs want "someone to do it for them" (service, not software)
- First sale takes 3-6 months of relationship building
- Face-to-face meetings are mandatory in Bulgarian business culture
- Single founder cannot simultaneously maintain 4 SaaS + do sales
- Consulting revenue is faster (no product-market fit delay)

## Risk Assessment

### High Probability Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Slow relationship building** | Revenue delay | 70% | Start networking immediately, multiple channels |
| **Bulgarian market price sensitivity** | Lower margins | 60% | Position on quality, not price |
| **Single founder capacity** | Burnout, delivery issues | 80% | Limit active clients to 10, consider contractor Q3 |

### Medium Probability Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Currency fluctuation (BGN/EUR)** | Margin impact | 40% | Price in EUR, bill in BGN |
| **Competitor price war** | Margin compression | 30% | Differentiate on credentials |
| **Client non-payment** | Cash flow | 25% | 50% upfront payment policy |

### Low Probability Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Regulatory change** | Service pivot | 15% | Monitor EU regulations |
| **Health/personal issues** | Business pause | 10% | No mitigation (single founder reality) |
