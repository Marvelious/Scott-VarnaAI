## About AI Marketing BG

AI Marketing BG is an AI-powered marketing agency launching in Varna, focusing on the Black Sea tourism industry. We help hotels reduce their dependency on OTAs (Booking.com) and increase direct bookings through AI-driven marketing strategies.

**What Makes Us Different:**
- AI-powered content and campaign optimization
- Performance guarantees (rare in Bulgaria)
- Focus on measurable ROI, not vanity metrics
- Western European service quality at Bulgarian pricing

---

## Role Overview

We're looking for a native Bulgarian speaker with sales/BD experience to be our face in the local market. You'll be responsible for building relationships with hotel managers, attending networking events, and closing deals.

**Why This Role Matters:**
The founder is a Western European digital marketing expert but does not speak Bulgarian. Bulgarian business culture requires face-to-face relationship building in the local language. You are CRITICAL to our success.

## Responsibilities

### Sales & Business Development (70%)
- Identify and qualify leads (hotels, real estate agencies)
- Conduct cold outreach (calls, emails, LinkedIn) in Bulgarian
- Schedule and attend coffee meetings with prospects
- Present services and close deals
- Maintain CRM with pipeline and activities

### Networking & Events (20%)
- Attend VCCI (Chamber of Commerce) monthly meetings
- Represent company at tourism and business events
- Build personal network in Varna business community
- Identify speaking opportunities for the team

### Client Communication (10%)
- Support client onboarding in Bulgarian
- Translate key materials and proposals
- Assist with client meetings when language support needed
