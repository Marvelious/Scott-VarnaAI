## Research Tasks

### Contact Information to Find
- [ ] General Manager name and email
- [ ] Marketing Director/Manager (if exists)
- [ ] Owner/Group owner
- [ ] LinkedIn profiles
- [ ] Direct phone numbers

### Sources for Research
1. **Hotel websites** - Contact pages, team pages
2. **LinkedIn** - Search by hotel name + "manager" + "Varna"
3. **TripAdvisor** - Sometimes lists GM names in responses
4. **Booking.com** - Property details
5. **VCCI directory** - Member hotels
6. **Bulgarian Hotel Association** - Member list
7. **Tourism trade shows** - Exhibitor lists

### Qualification Criteria
- ✅ Currently active on OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia)
- ✅ Has own website (not just OTA presence)
- ✅ 100+ rooms (budget for marketing)
- ✅ 4+ rating on TripAdvisor (quality signal)
- ✅ Active social media (indicates marketing awareness)
- ❌ Recently changed ownership (unstable)
- ❌ Under renovation (no budget)
- ❌ Seasonal-only (April-October)

но зависимостта от Booking.com вероятно изяжда 15-20% от приходите ви.

Предлагам безплатен 30-минутен анализ, който ще покаже:
• Къде губите директни резервации
• Какво правят конкурентите ви
• 3 бързи подобрения за тази седмица

Имате ли 15 минути за кафе следващата седмица?

С уважение,
[Име]
AI Marketing BG
