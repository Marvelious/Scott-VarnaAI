How to tell if your IT provider is actually secure (5 questions most companies forget to ask):

**Question 1**: "Can I see your ISO 27001 certificate?"
Red flag: "We follow ISO 27001 principles" (that means they're NOT certified)
Green flag: Current certificate with valid dates

**Question 2**: "What's your incident response time SLA?"
Red flag: "We'll handle it as fast as we can"
Green flag: Specific hours (e.g., "Critical issues: 2-hour response")

**Question 3**: "Where is my data physically stored, and who has access?"
Red flag: Vague answers about "the cloud"
Green flag: Specific data center locations, access logs, GDPR data processing agreements

**Question 4**: "Can you provide client references who had a security incident?"
Red flag: "We've never had an incident" (everyone has incidents)
Green flag: Transparent discussion of past issues and how they were resolved

**Question 5**: "What happens to my data if we stop working together?"
Red flag: No clear data deletion policy
Green flag: Written data retention and secure deletion procedures

Your IT provider holds the keys to your business. Make sure they're actually secure, not just saying the right words.
