**Research**:
- [ ] Company website reviewed (industry, size, products/services)

- [ ] Recent company news or announcements checked
- [ ] Identified 1-2 specific pain points likely relevant to their industry

**Technical Setup**:

- [ ] Case studies relevant to their industry ready to share (PDF links)

- [ ] Calendar (Calendly) ready to schedule follow-up

**Mindset**:
- [ ] This is a discovery conversation, not a sales pitch
- [ ] Listen 70%, talk 30%

### Discovery Questions (15 minutes)

**üéØ Goal**: Uncover pain points, urgency, budget awareness, decision process

#### **Section 1: Current Challenges (5 min)**

**AI Adoption**:
- "Are you currently using AI in your business, or is it something you're exploring?"
  - *If using*: "What's working well? What's frustrating?"
  - *If exploring*: "What's holding you back from adopting AI?"

**Security/Compliance Pain**:
- "What's your biggest challenge with security or compliance right now?"
- "Have you ever had a security incident or audit finding that was painful to deal with?"
- "Are you aware of the new NIS2 directive that came into effect in October 2024?" (Often: No)
  - *If No*: "It affects about 500-1,000 Bulgarian companies in critical sectors. Compliance deadline is October 2024. Are you in [critical infrastructure/digital services/healthcare/finance]?"

#### **Section 2: Business Impact (5 min)**

**Quantify Pain**:
- "If you could solve [specific challenge they mentioned], what would that mean for your business?"
- "What happens if you don't address this? What's the risk?" (GDPR fines, lost customers, audit failures, etc.)
- "Have you calculated the cost of compliance failures?" (e.g., GDPR fines up to 4% of global revenue)

**Timeline & Urgency**:
- "Is this something you need to address in Q1 2026, or is it more of a long-term goal?"
- "What would need to happen for this to become a top priority?" (Trigger events: audit scheduled, customer demand, regulatory deadline)

#### **Section 3: Decision Process (5 min)**

**Budget Awareness**:
- "Have you budgeted for security or compliance work this year?"
- "When you've engaged consultants before, what price range have you worked with?"
  - *(Frame expectations)*: "Just for context, my audit services start at ‚Ç¨980. Full implementations are in the ‚Ç¨4,900-‚Ç¨9,800 range depending on scope. Does that align with your expectations?"

**Decision Authority**:
- "Who else would be involved in a decision like this?" (IT Director, CFO, CEO?)
- "What's your typical process for engaging external consultants?" (RFP, informal quotes, single vendor?)
- "If we find a good fit today, what would the timeline look like from decision to kick-off?"
