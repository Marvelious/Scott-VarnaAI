>>> SEO ALT TEXT BEST PRACTICES
===========================================

✅ **DO:**
- Include focus keyword "AI Projektmanagement" in 2-3 main images
- Keep alt text descriptive and natural (50-125 characters)
- Describe what the image shows AND its business context
- Use German language for German-facing site
- Include relevant compliance terms (DSGVO, NIS2, BSI)
- Add certifications mentioned (ISO 27001, TISAX)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Keyword stuff (don't repeat "AI Projektmanagement" 10 times)
- Use generic alt text like "Image 1" or "Banner"
- Make alt text too long (>150 characters)
- Ignore screen reader accessibility
- Leave alt text empty (bad for SEO and accessibility)
- Use English alt text on German site
