## What Sintra CAN Automate

### 1. Content Generation
- Weekly batch of social media posts
- Multi-language support (German for ai-projektmanager.de, varna-agenten.de)
- Visual design suggestions
- Hashtag recommendations

### 2. Scheduling & Publishing
- Cross-platform posting (LinkedIn, FB, IG)
- Calendar view of scheduled content
- Bulk approval workflow

### 3. Engagement
- Comment monitoring (Facebook Commenter when enabled)
- Interaction tracking
- Performance analytics (reach, interactions)

### 4. Brand Consistency
- Brain AI learns your brand voice
- Personalized content based on uploaded snippets/media
- Consistent messaging across platforms
