## Product Hunt Best Practices

### Do's
- Post at 12:01 AM PT (product of the day starts then)
- Engage with every comment within first 4 hours
- Share on social media, email list, communities
- Ask existing customers to upvote and comment
- Prepare media kit in advance

### Don'ts
- Don't ask for upvotes directly (against TOS)
- Don't launch multiple products same day
- Don't ignore negative feedback
- Don't post and disappear

### Metrics to Track
- Upvotes (target: 100+ for visibility)
- Comments (engagement signals quality)
- Website traffic from Product Hunt
- Sign-ups during launch day
- Product Hunt ranking position

## Notes

### Brand Independence
- Each brand launches with its own identity
- No mention of "Classic Security EOOD" in product descriptions
- Legal entity only appears in Terms of Service/Privacy Policy
- Each brand has its own social media, email, and community presence

### Cross-Promotion (Subtle)
- After establishing independent presence, subtle cross-links
- "Also check out our other EU-compliant tools" in footer

- Joint webinars on GDPR/NIS2 topics
