## 12. Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Items

1. **Backlink Velocity**
   - Risk: 20-30 links/week might trigger Google scrutiny
   - Mitigation: Vary anchor text (40% brand, 20% URL, 20% generic, 20% keyword)
   - Mitigation: Gradual ramp-up, not sudden spikes

2. **Toxic Backlinks**
   - Risk: Automated tools may acquire low-quality links
   - Mitigation: Monthly Ahrefs audits to identify toxic links
   - Mitigation: Prefer semi-automated tools with human oversight

3. **Resource Constraints**
   - Risk: 25-35 hours/month required
   - Mitigation: Prioritize critical tasks (backlinks, GBPs)
   - Mitigation: Use automation to reduce manual effort

### Medium-Risk Items

4. **Google Algorithm Updates**
   - Risk: SEO strategies may need adjustment
   - Mitigation: Monitor Search Console for ranking changes
   - Mitigation: Diversify traffic sources (social, direct, referral)

5. **Budget Overruns**
   - Risk: Tools may cost more than expected
   - Mitigation: Start with minimum budget, scale based on results
   - Mitigation: Use one-time purchases vs subscriptions
