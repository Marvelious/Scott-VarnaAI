## Email Best Practices

### DO:
- ✅ Personalize company name and sector
- ✅ Keep under 150 words
- ✅ One clear CTA (book meeting)
- ✅ Mention free assessment
- ✅ Use Bulgarian business tone (formal but friendly)

### DON'T:
- ❌ Oversell features
- ❌ Use fear tactics ("You'll be fined!")
- ❌ Send PDFs in first email (too heavy)
- ❌ Ask multiple questions
- ❌ Make it about you (focus on their problem)
