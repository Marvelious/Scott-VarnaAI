## Technical Specifications

### Card Dimensions
- **Standard**: 85mm Ã— 55mm (3.5" Ã— 2")
- **Orientation**: Horizontal (landscape)
- **Corners**: Rounded (2mm radius) OR square (professional choice)

### Print Specifications
- **Paper Stock**: 350gsm cardstock (premium feel)
- **Finish**:
  - Option 1: Matte (professional, easy to write on)
  - Option 2: Soft-touch laminate (luxury feel)
  - Recommended: **Matte** for business context
- **Print**: Full color CMYK, both sides
- **Bleed**: 3mm bleed on all sides (important for professional printing)

### File Requirements for Printer
- **Format**: PDF (high-resolution, 300 DPI minimum)
- **Color Mode**: CMYK (not RGB)
- **Fonts**: Embedded or outlined
- **Size**: 91mm Ã— 61mm (includes 3mm bleed on all sides)

## Design Principles

### Professional Standards
1. **Readability**: All text clearly legible at arm's length
2. **Hierarchy**: Most important info (name, title) largest
3. **White Space**: Don't overcrowd - less is more
4. **Consistency**: Match VarnaAI brand colors if available
5. **Contact Priority**: Make email and phone easy to find

### Cultural Considerations (Bulgaria)
- Professional, not flashy (Bulgarian business culture values substance)
- Clear credentials and certifications (ISO, CCIE, Azure)
- Include physical location (Varna) for local credibility
- Both Bulgarian and international credentials acceptable

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- âŒ Too much text (cramped, hard to read)
- âŒ Font size too small (<10pt on back side)
- âŒ Low contrast (light text on light background)
- âŒ Misaligned elements (looks unprofessional)
- âŒ RGB color mode (prints poorly, use CMYK)
- âŒ Forgetting bleed (white edges after cutting)
