## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Access Google Business Profile
1. Go to https://business.google.com/
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Add your business to Google"

### Step 2: For Each Brand
1. Enter business name exactly as shown above
2. Select primary category from dropdown
3. Add additional categories (up to 9 more)
4. Enter address
5. Define service areas
6. Add phone number (use exact format: +359 88 252 1755)
7. Add website URL
8. Paste description (check character count)
9. Upload logo (400x400px minimum)
10. Add cover photos
11. Set business hours: Mon-Fri 09:00-17:00

### Step 3: Verification
- Choose verification method (postcard, phone, or email)
- Complete verification process
- Wait for approval (1-14 days for postcard)

### Step 4: Social Media Links
After verification, add all social media profiles from the links above.
