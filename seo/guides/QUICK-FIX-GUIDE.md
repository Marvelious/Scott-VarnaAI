# Quick Fix Guide - 3 Minutes Total

## Fix 1: Services Page Placeholder (1 minute)

1. Go to: **https://varnaai.com/secure-ai-services-gdpr/**
2. Click **"Edit Page"** in top admin bar
3. Press **Ctrl+F** (Find)
4. Search for: **"Make an impact"**
5. Click on that paragraph block
6. Press **Delete** key
7. Click **"Update"** button (top right)

**Done!** ✅

---

## Fix 2: Pricing Page Placeholder (1 minute)

1. Go to: **https://varnaai.com/secure-ai-saas-pricing/**
2. Click **"Edit Page"** in top admin bar
3. Press **Ctrl+F** (Find)
4. Search for: **"Consider using this"**

## Fix 3: RetirementAI Contact Form Dropdowns (1 minute)

1. Go to: **https://varnaai.com/retirementai-portfolio/**
2. Click **"Edit Page"** in top admin bar
3. Scroll to bottom contact form section
4. Click on **first dropdown** that says "Option 1"
5. In right sidebar → **Field Settings** → **Label**
6. Change to: **"I'm interested in..."**
7. In **Options** section, replace with:
   - Demo Request
   - Partnership Opportunity
   - Technical Questions
   - Investment Inquiry

8. Click on **second dropdown** that says "Option 2"
9. Change label to: **"Company Size"**
10. Replace options with:
    - Freelancer / Solopreneur
    - Small Business (1-10 employees)
    - Medium Business (11-50 employees)
    - Enterprise (50+ employees)

11. Click **"Update"** button (top right)

**Done!** ✅
