## New Pages to Create (Priority 1)

### 1. `/get-quote/` - Quote Request Page
**Focus Keyword**: AI Marketing Quote Bulgaria
**Target**: Clients ready to buy AI marketing services
**Purpose**: Capture high-intent leads with personalized quote form

**Page Elements**:
- Quote request form (name, company, email, budget, timeline)
- Service selection (AI Content, AI Analytics, AI Automation, Full Service)
- Response time commitment ("We respond within 24 hours")
- Trust signals (GDPR badge, client logos)
- FAQ: Common questions about pricing and process

### 2. `/roi-calculator/` - ROI Calculator Page
**Focus Keyword**: AI Marketing ROI Calculator
**Target**: Budget-conscious decision makers
**Purpose**: Show value of AI marketing investment

**Page Elements**:
- Interactive ROI calculator
- Input fields: Current marketing spend, team size, manual hours
- Output: Projected savings, time recovered, revenue potential
- Comparison: AI vs. traditional marketing costs
- CTA: "Get Your Custom ROI Report"

### 3. `/gdpr-ai-marketing/` - GDPR Compliance Page
**Focus Keyword**: GDPR Compliant AI Marketing
**Target**: EU businesses concerned about data privacy
**Purpose**: Address compliance concerns, build trust

**Page Elements**:
- GDPR compliance certification
- Data processing transparency
- Bulgarian/EU data residency commitment
- Privacy-by-design principles
- Comparison: US AI tools vs. EU-compliant solutions
