## New Pages to Create (Priority 1)

### 1. `/demo-anfragen/` - Demo Request Page
**Focus Keyword**: KI Agenten Demo Kreativagentur
**Target Audience**: Agency owners evaluating AI solutions
**Purpose**: Capture high-intent leads with personalized demo booking

**Page Elements**:
- Demo booking widget (Calendly/HubSpot)
- Industry-specific use cases (Design, Marketing, Advertising)
- 3 demo formats: Live, Video, Sandbox
- Trust signals: Agency client logos (anonymized)
- FAQ: Common agency questions about AI integration

### 2. `/preise-kreativagenturen/` - Creative Agency Pricing
**Focus Keyword**: KI Tools Preise Kreativagentur
**Target Audience**: Budget-conscious agency decision makers
**Purpose**: Show value vs. traditional creative tools

**Page Elements**:
- Pricing comparison table (vs. Adobe Suite, Canva Pro, traditional methods)
- ROI calculator for agencies (time saved × hourly rate)
- Package options: Solo, Team, Enterprise
- Case study snippet: "Agentur XY spart 40 Stunden/Monat"
- Money-back guarantee

### 3. `/ki-tools-vergleich/` - AI Tools Comparison
**Focus Keyword**: KI Tools Vergleich Agenturen
**Target Audience**: Agencies comparing solutions
**Purpose**: Position as best choice for creative agencies

**Page Elements**:
- Feature comparison matrix
- Competitor analysis (generic AI vs. agency-specific)
- Workflow integration benefits
- DSGVO-compliance advantage
- German support advantage
