## Notes for Big Dick

1. **Personalization is key** - Generic pitches get ignored
2. **Start with Tier 3** - Build writing samples and confidence
3. **Follow up** - 70% of acceptances come from follow-up
4. **Quality over quantity** - Better to get 5 high-authority links than 20 low-quality
5. **Leverage CV experience** - Gennadius's real experience is the differentiator
6. **Track everything** - Use the spreadsheet template above

**Expected Results**:
- 20-30 pitches sent
- 8-12 articles accepted
- 10-15 DoFollow backlinks
- Domain authority boost: +5-10 points per site
