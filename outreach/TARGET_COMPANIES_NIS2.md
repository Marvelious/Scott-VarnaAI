## How to Fill This List

### Step 1: Find Companies

**Free Methods:**
- Google: `"[sector] GmbH" site:linkedin.com/company`
- LinkedIn Company Search: Filter by industry, size (51-200, 201-500), location Germany
- Wer liefert was (wlw.de) - German B2B directory
- Firmenwissen.de - Company database
- Bundesanzeiger - Public company filings
- IHK company directories (regional)

**Search queries:**
```
"Maschinenbau GmbH" 50-200 Mitarbeiter
"Medizintechnik" Unternehmen Deutschland
"Logistik GmbH" NRW Bayern Baden-Württemberg
"Energieversorger" Stadtwerke Mittelstand
"Rechenzentrum" "Data Center" Deutschland GmbH
```

### Step 2: Find Decision Makers

**LinkedIn search within company:**
- IT-Leiter
- Leiter IT
- Head of IT
- CISO
- Informationssicherheitsbeauftragter
- IT-Sicherheit
- Datenschutzbeauftragter
- CTO
- Geschäftsführer Technik

**If no LinkedIn:**
- Check company website "Team" / "Über uns" / "Ansprechpartner"
- Call reception: "Wer ist bei Ihnen für IT-Sicherheit zuständig?"
- Xing profile search

### Step 3: Get Email

**Email patterns to try:**
- vorname.nachname@company.de
- v.nachname@company.de
- nachname@company.de
- vorname@company.de

**Verify with:**
- hunter.io (free tier)
- Verify email tools
- LinkedIn "Contact info" (if connected)

## Prioritization Criteria

**Hot prospects (contact first):**
- Recently in news for security incident
- Hiring for IT security roles (= knows they have gap)
- New IT-Leiter (wants to make impact)
- In highly regulated sub-sector (medical devices, energy)
- Recently grew past 50 employees

**Warm prospects:**
- Standard Mittelstand, no visible security focus
- Active on LinkedIn (will see your profile)
- Have website with contact forms

**Cold prospects (lower priority):**
- Already have big consulting firm logos on site
- Large IT department visible
- Publicly traded (different buying process)

## Regional Focus

**Best regions for Mittelstand density:**
1. Baden-Württemberg (manufacturing)
2. Bayern (automotive, tech)
3. NRW (industry, logistics)
4. Hessen (finance, pharma)
5. Niedersachsen (automotive suppliers)

**Consider proximity to you (Varna, Bulgaria):**
- Remote consulting works fine
- But being able to say "Ich komme gerne vorbei" for important meetings is valuable
- Focus on companies open to remote consultants (post-COVID this is most)
